Welcome to the Well-Known Boreholes website

At Well-Known Boreholes, we want everyone to have access to clean and safe water, which will enhance their health and standard of living. Our goals are to build boreholes in regions where there is a lack of access to drinkable and safe water, to educate communities on conservation practices and hygiene and to work with other groups to increase access to water in impoverished regions.
